General information

FastQTL is a QTL mapper with several notable features:

  • Fast; with a permutation scheme relying on Beta approximation. No need to perform millions of permutations to reach low significance levels, only 100 is needed!
  • Flexible; various correlation measures (Pearson & Spearman) are implemented w/o qualitative/quantitative covariates
  • User-friendly; only standard file formats are used and easy-to-use options implemented.
  • Cluster-friendly; parallelization is easy to set up for large deployment on compute clusters.

FastQTL is developed by Olivier DELANEAU, Halit ONGEN and Alfonso BUIL & Manolis DERMITZAKIS.
FastQTL is a project developed in the FunPopGen lab.
